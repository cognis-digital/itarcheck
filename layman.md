`itarcheck` scans your code, documents, and datasheets for words and phrases that signal potential U.S. export-control issues — things like missile guidance systems, military electronics, or encryption technology that may be regulated under ITAR or EAR law. It works like a spell-checker but for compliance risk: point it at a folder, and it lists every flagged term with its location and a plain-English explanation of why it matters. It is aimed at engineers, technical writers, and compliance teams who need a fast, automated first pass before sharing software or documentation externally.
